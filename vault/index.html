What this tracks

Verified issuers, native units, no USD interpretation

The issuer map is operator-curated and reconciled against on-chain state directly, not pulled from a third-party aggregator. Each issuer is included only after wallet, currency, and authority flags are independently confirmed.

For OpenEden TBL, the displayed "circulating" reflects supply actually distributed to holders, with treasury-held inventory excluded. Public aggregators report the unfiltered total and overstate the figure substantially.

For Justoken JMWH, the figure is in megawatt-hours, not dollars. The marketed USD valuation depends on an off-chain energy price feed we don't carry; the on-chain unit count is what we report.

Mints and burns are picked up in near real time as XRPL ledgers close. The dashboard refreshes on a short cadence so newly minted supply or holder redemptions land here within minutes of settling on-chain.

Daily snapshots run once per UTC day per live issuer, capturing outstanding supply, net circulating, trustline count, and the day-over-day delta against the prior snapshot.

Issuers in rails-only status (Archax/abrdn) have their permissioned-trustline rails configured but zero tokens minted on mainnet so far. They're polled to capture the first mint when it lands.

AMM pools where one side is an RWA issuer's IOU are surfaced alongside the issuers. Pool balances refresh on the same daily cadence as the issuer snapshots; pair tiles rank by RWA-side magnitude so meaningful liquidity floats above dust pools.
