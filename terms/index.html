Static summary (always available)

Pricing and finality

Each XR-Sentinel scan and each XR-Telemetry snapshot is priced at $0.10 USD, payable in XRP. Drops are computed at request time from the live XRP/USD spot, so the advertised USD amount stays accurate as the spot moves. Successful payloads are paid in full; payment is final on settlement. If a request fails before settlement (verify-then-work-then-settle is enforced on both services), no charge occurs and the presigned transaction is never broadcast. The 30-minute payment freshness window applies to web invoices; the 600-second window applies to x402.

What we provide

XR-Sentinel returns behavioral pattern classifications for XRPL accounts. XR-Telemetry returns an XRPL macro telemetry snapshot. Both are best-effort, on-chain-derived data products. Sanity bounds and per-field provenance are declared in each service's /agents.json and /schema. A field's value reads from a sanity-bounded cache or the appropriate fallback when the cache is stale; the cache state is exposed at /healthz.

What we do not provide

Neither service is a risk score, AML or sanctions determination, compliance product, investment recommendation, trading signal, price prediction, or financial advice. The Burst Math output in XR-Telemetry is a sensitivity tool that shows what the math implies under explicit operator assumptions; it is not a forecast. Always do your own research before transacting.
