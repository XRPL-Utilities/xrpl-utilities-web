XR-Lineage™

Trace the flow, understand the origin

Live lineage tracker for institutional XRPL movement. Watch fresh RLUSD mint and burn activity, see auto-discovered counterparties as Ripple onboards them, and catch multi-hop relay bursts the moment they fire on-chain. Powered by the XR-Pulse detection layer.

Live detection · anonymized public view · powered by XR-Pulse

Three detection layers

Counterparty discovery

A background loop polls Ripple-controlled wallets at a recurring interval and scores every new outgoing-Payment destination against a weighted on-chain heuristic stack. Candidates that clear the score threshold AND show at least one provenance signal (RLUSD pre-approval test, multi- exchange connectivity, or Ripple-funded-MM inflow) are auto-promoted into the runtime watchlist. Personal whales don't auto-promote on capital alone.

Relay-burst detection

Multi-hop chain detector that links consecutive whale Payments where one hop's receiver is the next hop's sender, same currency, within a one-hour window. Fires when a chain crosses three hops with twenty million dollars total movement, shows the pure-payment relay fingerprint, and anchors on at least one labeled wallet. Every unlabeled hop in a detected chain gets auto-promoted with a synthesized label.

Focus watch

Per-wallet outgoing-Payment alerter that fires on every new outgoing transaction at any value, regardless of USD floor. Fills the gap between the whale-grade watcher and the multi- hop chain detector for the case where a labeled wallet is sitting on a large fresh balance and starts moving it in small chunks. Default seed is the terminal recipients from the most recent mint chain.

Methodology and limits

What we measure

On-chain Payments, Trustlines, and Escrow activity from the validated XRPL ledger. RLUSD obligations from the issuer's gateway_balances at the live ledger. Auto-discovered candidates are scored on a weighted heuristic stack combining balance, account age, on-chain provenance signals, and cluster fingerprints. Relay bursts are detected from sequenced whale Payments meeting the configured thresholds. Detection is deterministic and reproducible.

What we don't claim

The wallets surfaced here are heuristic candidates, not confirmed identities. Auto-promotion attaches a synthesized label for downstream event enrichment; it is not an endorsement, regulatory determination, or investment recommendation. We are not affiliated with Ripple Labs. Use this surface as one input among many. The full attributed wallet list is intentionally not exposed publicly. DYOR.
